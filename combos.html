    Character Overview

    Kazuya Mishima is a high risk, high reward character. His gameplay revolves around playing mind games with his opponent by setting up 50|50s and baiting counter hits. With his limited move set, Kazuya players are expected to have strong fundamentals of the game. His electric is one of the best whiff punishers and launchers in the game.
